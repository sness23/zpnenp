3.1 Subset Sum in NP

Theorem 3.1.1 Subset Sum \(\in \NP \)

subsetSum_in_NP def:subset_sum \(\mathrm{SubsetSum}(S, t) \iff \exists S' \in \mathcal{P}(S),\; \sum _{x \in S'} x = t\). Verification is decidable (polynomial-time checkable).

Theorem 3.1.2 Subset Sum Decidable

subsetSum_decidable thm:subset_sum_np The Subset Sum predicate has a Decidable instance.
