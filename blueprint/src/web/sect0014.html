3.4 Structure vs. Computation

Theorem 3.4.1 Structure–Computation Dichotomy

structure_vs_computation_dichotomy thm:doubling_gt_one For non-trivial \(A \subseteq \ZZ /p\ZZ \): either \(|A+A| {\gt} |A|\) (growing sumset) or \(|A+A| \leq K|A|\) (Freiman structure). The adversary cannot avoid both.

Theorem 3.4.2 Adversary Full Dichotomy

adversary_full_dichotomy thm:struct_comp_dichotomy, thm:sum_product Every non-trivial instance simultaneously has growing sumsets AND large sum-product quantity: \(|A| {\lt} |A+A|\) and \(|A| {\lt} \max (|A+A|, |A \cdot A|)\).
