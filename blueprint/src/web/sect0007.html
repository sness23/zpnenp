2.2 Sumset Bounds

Theorem 2.2.1 Cauchy–Davenport

For \(A, B \subseteq \ZZ /p\ZZ \) with \(p\) prime: \(|A + B| \geq \min (p,\; |A| + |B| - 1)\).
