2.3 Sum-Product Phenomenon

Theorem 2.3.1 Sum-Product Growth

bourgain_katz_tao thm:doubling_gt_one For \(A \subseteq \ZZ /p\ZZ \) with \(2 \leq |A| \leq p/2\): \(|A| {\lt} \max (|A + A|, |A \cdot A|)\).
