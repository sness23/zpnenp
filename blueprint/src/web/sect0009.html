3 Complexity Bridge (Planned)

Theorem 3.0.1 Subset Sum \(\in \NP \)

def:subset_sum Subset Sum is in \(\NP \): given a subset \(S'\), the verifier checks \(\sum _{x \in S'} x = t\) in polynomial time.

Theorem 3.0.2 \(\PP \neq \NP \)

thm:inverse_davenport, thm:inverse_egz, thm:density_dichotomy, thm:subset_sum_np Goal. No polynomial-time algorithm solves Subset Sum in the worst case.

Strategy: Use the structural characterization of hard instances from zero-sum theory to show that any polynomial-time algorithm must fail on instances at critical density with intermediate structure.
